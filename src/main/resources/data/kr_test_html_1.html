Etymology 1

Sino-Korean word from 正義 (“justice”).

Noun

정의 • (jeong'ui) (hanja 正義)

Korean Wikipedia has an article on:
정의 (타로)
Wikipedia ko
  1. justice
    Synonym: 의리(義理) (uiri)
    Antonyms: 불의(不義) (burui), 부정(不正) (bujeong)
  2. (tarot) Justice (Tarot card)
Derived terms
  • 정의(正義)롭다 (jeong'uiropda, “righteous; rightful”)

Etymology 2

Sino-Korean word from 定義 (“define”).

Noun

정의 • (jeong'ui) (hanja 定義)

  1. definition
Derived terms
  • 정의(定義)하다 (jeong'uihada, “to define”)
  • 정의(定義)되다 (jeong'uidoeda, “to be defined”)
